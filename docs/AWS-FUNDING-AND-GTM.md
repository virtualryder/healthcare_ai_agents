# AWS Funding & Go-To-Market — HPP AI Agent Suite

This document explains, at a healthcare-SI level of accuracy, how AWS partner-funding and
co-sell programs can underwrite an HPP pilot and how the suite reaches a customer through
AWS Marketplace. Program names, eligibility, and benefits change over time and are
AWS-discretionary — the descriptions below are directional. **Do not quote specific dollar
figures or funding percentages to a customer**; confirm current terms with your AWS Partner
Development Manager (PDM) and Partner Solutions Architect (PSA) for the specific opportunity.

## Who funds a pilot — the program landscape

### AWS MAP (Migration Acceleration Program — including Modernization)
MAP is AWS's structured program for migrating and modernizing workloads, organized in
**Assess → Mobilize → Migrate & Modernize** phases. For this suite, the modernization
motion is the relevant one: standing up the governed agent platform on AWS-native services
(Bedrock, Lambda, Step Functions, DynamoDB, S3) is a modernization workload. MAP can provide
**AWS-funded credits and partner investment** tied to assessed/committed spend and to hitting
the program's milestones. MAP typically requires a qualified opportunity, an assessment, and
a committed migration/modernization plan.

### Proof-of-Concept (PoC) / Proof-of-Value funding
For early-stage opportunities, AWS offers **PoC / Proof-of-Concept funding** (sometimes
surfaced through the Partner Funding portal as PoC or "Proof of Concept" investment) to
offset the cost of a time-boxed pilot — for example, deploying one or two HPP agents in
fixture/limited-live mode to demonstrate the governed pattern and quantify ROI. PoC funding
is well matched to this suite's maturity (Demonstrated → Deployable): the pilot proves the
governed pattern before the customer commits to production hardening.

### ISV Accelerate / partner co-sell funding
If the SI's offering is listed and the partner is in the relevant AWS Partner programs,
**ISV Accelerate** provides co-sell support — AWS seller alignment, opportunity sharing
through APN Customer Engagements (ACE), and reduced Marketplace listing fees on co-sold
deals. This is the path to get AWS account teams actively co-selling the suite into their
healthcare accounts rather than merely funding a pilot. Partner-funding paths (such as
Partner Originated/Sourced investment) attach to qualified ACE opportunities.

### Marketplace private offer mechanics
AWS Marketplace lets the SI transact the suite (and the engagement services) through a
**private offer** — a negotiated, customer-specific price, term, and EULA/contract that the
buyer accepts in their own AWS account. Mechanically:
- The offer can be structured as a SaaS/usage listing or a **professional-services / custom
  private offer** for the engagement (validation, IdP integration, live-connector work).
- Spend through Marketplace **counts toward the customer's AWS commitment** (e.g., EDP/PPA),
  which is often the deciding factor for a healthcare buyer with a committed-spend agreement.
- **Channel partner private offers (CPPO)** let a reseller or the SI transact on behalf of
  the ISV, supporting multi-party deals common in healthcare.
- The private offer carries the contract terms, so the BAA/security exhibits and the
  professional-services SOW can be referenced or attached as part of the transaction.

## How the programs stack across the deal lifecycle

| Stage | Motion | Likely funding path |
|---|---|---|
| Qualify | Register the opportunity in ACE | ISV Accelerate co-sell alignment |
| Prove | Time-boxed pilot (1–2 agents, fixture/limited-live) | PoC / Proof-of-Concept funding |
| Commit | Modernization plan + committed spend | AWS MAP (Modernize) investment |
| Transact | Customer buys suite + services | Marketplace private offer (counts to commitment) |
| Scale | Roll out additional agents to production | MAP milestones + co-sell continuation |

## What to do next on a live opportunity

1. Engage the AWS **PDM/PSA** early and register the opportunity in **ACE** — co-sell and
   funding both flow from a qualified ACE opportunity.
2. Match the funding ask to the stage: **PoC funding** to prove, **MAP** to modernize and
   commit.
3. Plan the **Marketplace private offer** so the transaction counts toward the customer's
   AWS commitment and carries the BAA/SOW terms.
4. Confirm current program names, eligibility, and amounts with AWS — treat everything here
   as directional, not a quote.
